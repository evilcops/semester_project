Our Approach

We reject the notion that you need to attract all the clients.

Instead, it’s all about targeting the right audience for your small or solo law firm with laser-focused positioning. Our experience working with lawyers from different disciplines tells us that success for solo and small firms is all about the quality of lead your website brings in—that’s where we help.

Our law firm websites are based on both the Squarespace and WordPress platforms, and we’re here to help you figure out the right solution for your practice.

Who We Help
